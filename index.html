The Arab Fashion Council has established the Institute of Innovative Fashion (IIF) to foster creativity and excellence in the fashion industry. Offering specialized programs, practical training, and collaborations with industry leaders, the IIF empowers aspiring fashion professionals and provides opportunities to make a notable impact in Arab fashion and beyond.

AFC GREEN LABEL

SUPPORTING SUSTAINABLE DESIGNERS

The Arab Fashion Council launched the AFC GREEN LABEL during Arab Fashion Week in May 2018 to address the fashion industry’s contribution to global warming. The initiative promotes an environmentally responsible approach by emphasizing the use of ethical materials and aligning with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.
